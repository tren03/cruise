Cruise Man Pages

Cruise is a browser extension designed to streamline tab management, navigation, and enhance productivity for power users. With Cruise, users can quickly navigate between open tabs, access frequently visited websites, search through their browser history, and manage bookmarks â€” all from a single, intuitive interface.
[It also changes the theme based on your preffered browser theme]

Modes

  • Force Search: Shift + enter
    Search the query on the web, in any mode, regardless of the options shown.
  • Active Tab Mode: Alt + t
    Search and navigate between active tabs. This is the default mode. If the tab you want to navigate to is not in your current window, Cruise will bring the window containing the tab into focus, and navigate to the requested tab.
  • History Mode: Alt + h
    Search through the last 100 visited sites and navigate to a URL by selecting or pressing enter.
  • Bookmark Mode: Alt + b
    Search your saved bookmarks
  • Frequent Mode: Alt + f
    Search your most frequently visited websites

Commands

  • Change Theme: /c
    Change the appearance of Cruise by selecting from a range of available themes.
  • Man Pages: /h
    Bring up the Cruise Help pages
